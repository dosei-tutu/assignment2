Got Milk?

Milk Production in the USA

By Demetria Osei-Tutu

November 21, 2021

A picture of milk from https://www.nytimes.com/2020/03/14/style/milk-dairy-marketing.html?

In the age of veganism, there are a lot of non-dairy milk options: almond, oat, coconut and soy. A lot more option then what was seen a few years ago. According to Forbes, oat milk prices surged up 208% from 2019. It’s 2020 sales were $249 million. Almond milk is still the number one milk out of the non-dairy milk with 1.4 billion in sales in 2020 according to the website EatThis. Although non-dairy milks are providing cow's milk some competition, milk consumption is still high. Milk consumption actually increased in the span of 5 years. From 2015- 2020, milk production increased. It’s not a super large increase where it’s noticeable but it still increased. There is still a business for cows milk as the data shows. The surge of non-dairy milk does not mean cow’s milk is going anywhere anytime soon. It would seem America has got milk.
